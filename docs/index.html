The Expo Line and Crime

The Metro Expo Line Extension to Santa Monica opened on May 20th, 2016, connecting Santa Monica to downtown Los Angeles. Now that the line has been open for more than a year and a half, the city asked us to help them understand the impact of the line on crime.

We first looked at overall crime trends in the city, using a 90-day rolling average to smooth day to day variations while preserving the overall trend. As the chart on the right illustrates, there appears to be a potential uptick in crime after the line, so we took a deeper look into the types of crime driving the uptick.

From this analysis, it was clear that larceny crimes showed a marked increase post opening of the expo line, especially those closer to Expo Line stations.
